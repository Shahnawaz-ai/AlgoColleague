Algo Colleague

Connect your LinkedIn

To ensure your account's safety and avoid LinkedIn rate limits, you'll need to create a free personal developer app. It takes exactly 2 minutes.

1

Create a Developer App

Go to the LinkedIn Developer Portal and click Create app.

2

Request Permissions

Under the Products tab, request access to Sign In with LinkedIn using OpenID Connect and Share on LinkedIn.

3

Add Redirect URL

Under the Auth tab, add this exact URL to your Authorized redirect URLs:

https://algo-colleague.vercel.app/connect
4

Copy your Keys

Under the Auth tab, copy your Client ID and Client Secret into the form on the right.

App Credentials

Enter the keys from your new LinkedIn app.
