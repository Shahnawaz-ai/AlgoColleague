Step-by-Step Guide
1
Create a LinkedIn Developer App
Go to the LinkedIn Developer Portal and create a new application. Give it any name (e.g., "My Algo Colleague") and upload any logo. This is completely free.

Open LinkedIn Developer Portal →
2
Request "Share on LinkedIn" Product
Inside your new app, go to the Products tab. Find "Share on LinkedIn" and click Request access. Approval is usually instant.
3
Add the Redirect URL
Go to the Auth tab. Under "Authorized redirect URLs", add exactly:
http://localhost:3000/api/auth/callback
Click Add then Update.
4
Copy Your Client ID & Secret
Still on the Auth tab, copy your Client ID and Client Secret. Keep the secret safe — paste both into the form on the right. ➜
5
Click "Connect with LinkedIn"
After pasting your credentials and saving them, click the blue Connect with LinkedIn button. You'll be redirected to LinkedIn to authorize the app. After approval, you'll land in your dashboard!
✓
Successfully Connected!
Your LinkedIn account has been linked to Algo Colleague. You're all set to start scheduling posts and growing your presence.
