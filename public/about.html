About Algo Colleague

Algo Colleague was founded in 2026 with a single, clear mission: to give B2B creators, founders, and marketing agencies full control over their LinkedIn automation strategies without sacrificing their data privacy or their account security.

The Problem We're Solving

Before Algo Colleague, if you wanted to schedule a month of LinkedIn content, automate your commenting strategy, or deeply analyze your impressions, you had to hand over your LinkedIn authentication tokens to third-party SaaS platforms. These platforms ran on centralized servers, meaning if they suffered a data breach, your entire LinkedIn identity was at risk.

Furthermore, cloud-based tools often trigger LinkedIn's automated security flags because hundreds of accounts are posting from the same IP ranges. We realized there had to be a better, more secure way for professionals to grow their audience.

Our Solution: Self-Hosted Automation

We built Algo Colleague as a deploy-it-yourself (DIY) software suite. By providing the codebase directly to you, we empower you to host the tool on your own infrastructure—whether that's a free Vercel account, your own local machine, or a dedicated private server.

  • Total Privacy: Your authentication tokens and scheduled content never touch our servers. They are stored securely in your own environment.
  • Reduced Risk: Because requests originate from your own trusted servers or edge networks, you avoid the IP-flagging traps of traditional automation tools.
  • Unlimited Scale: You aren't boxed in by arbitrary SaaS limits. You own the infrastructure.
