✦ FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about using Algo Colleague safely and effectively.

Is Algo Colleague safe for my LinkedIn account?
Yes! Unlike cloud-based automation tools that share IP addresses and get flagged by LinkedIn, Algo Colleague is self-hosted. It runs on your own server or machine using your own IP address and the official LinkedIn API, which makes it incredibly secure and compliant.
Can I schedule PDF carousels or multi-image posts?
Absolutely. Algo Colleague supports text, single images, multi-image galleries, and PDF carousel posts. Our composer gives you a live preview so you know exactly how it will look before it goes live.
Do I need to leave my computer on?
If you deploy Algo Colleague to a small server (like a $5 DigitalOcean droplet), it runs 24/7 in the background. If you run it locally on your laptop, your computer needs to be on for the cron job to trigger the scheduled posts.
What happens if my connection token expires?
LinkedIn API tokens generally last 60 days. Algo Colleague will automatically alert you inside the dashboard when your token is nearing expiration, and you can securely reconnect with one click without losing any of your scheduled content.
Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
Yes! We offer a full 14-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you are not completely satisfied with how Algo Colleague automates your LinkedIn growth, just send us an email and we will refund your purchase immediately.
Can I manage multiple LinkedIn accounts?
Our Pro plan supports a single active LinkedIn account, which is perfect for solo creators. If you need to manage multiple profiles (up to 5), you can upgrade to our Agency plan, which includes bulk scheduling and unified analytics.
